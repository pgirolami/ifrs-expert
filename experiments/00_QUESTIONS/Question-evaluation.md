# From Chat GPT

## Highest-priority five
### Q6 — pouvoir sur une entité

**Why it matters**
- Big, central IFRS topic: control / consolidation
- Likely exercises IFRS 10 in a way very different from IFRS 9
- Good interview signal because it is concept-heavy and judgment-heavy, not mechanical

**What it tests**
- New standard family
- Structured factors / indicators / decision framework
- Whether Prompt A can identify the right top-level models without collapsing into vague “analysis”


### Q9 — licences de propriété intellectuelle : à une date donnée ou étalé

**Why it matters**

- Strong IFRS 15 question with a clear but nontrivial bifurcation
- Very good test of whether the pipeline handles a standard with lots of application guidance / examples
- Good business recognizability

**What it tests**

- Point-in-time vs over-time / right-to-access vs right-to-use type distinctions
- Retrieval of core rule + application guidance
- Whether answer style can stay crisp on a practical FAQ-style question

### Q12 — contrat ≤ 1 an renouvelable tacitement : short-term lease exemption?

**Why it matters**

- Strong IFRS 16 boundary question
- Excellent for testing whether your new reference/section-expansion logic helps with “definition plus practical edge case”
- Concrete and interview-friendly

**What it tests**

- Contract term / enforceable period / short-term exemption logic
- Mechanical-but-not-trivial reasoning
- Ability to retrieve governing paragraphs, not just explanatory text

### Q16 — engagement volontaire de neutralité carbone : provision?

**Why it matters**

- Strong IAS 37 / IFRIC-style judgment question
- Feels modern and practically relevant
- Likely to surface whether the system can separate:
   - commitment
   - communication
   - present obligation
   - future intention

**What it tests**

- Provision logic outside financial instruments / revenue / leases
- Whether the system handles “sounds important but may still be no provision” questions cleanly
-  A very different reasoning shape from Q1–Q3

### Q7 — transaction downstream avec entreprise associée / JV mise en équivalence

**Why it matters**

- Strong IAS 28 / equity method question
- Good test of a more technical, less FAQ-obvious area
- High signal if it works, because this is exactly the kind of thing that is easy for systems to get wrong

**What it tests**

- Equity method mechanics
- Partial elimination / profit recognition logic
- Whether retrieval can surface the right paragraphs in a denser standard area
