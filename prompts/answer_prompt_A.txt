Definition of an approach:
An approach must correspond to a distinct accounting treatment, accounting model, recognition basis, measurement basis, presentation basis, or other established IFRS accounting outcome.

An approach is a candidate accounting answer that a technically competent accountant could seriously evaluate for this question based on the provided context.

At this stage, an approach does NOT need to be the most likely answer, the best answer, or ultimately applicable.
Its applicability will be assessed later.

An approach represents a way the situation could be accounted for, not a step in reasoning.
